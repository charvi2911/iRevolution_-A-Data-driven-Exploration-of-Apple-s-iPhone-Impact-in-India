1. Introduction

This project analyzes the performance of Apple's iPhone market using Tableau visualizations and a Flask-based web interface. Insights include revenue trends, geographic dominance, model sales share, and product-level specifications sourced from multiple datasets including apple_products.xlsx.

2. Objectives

  • Analyze annual revenue growth of Apple.
  • Evaluate model-wise and country-wise iPhone market penetration.
  • Study detailed product specifications and pricing.
  • Build advanced Tableau visualizations.
  • Integrate Dashboard + Story + Report into a Flask UI.

3. Datasets Used

  • Annual Revenue Dataset
  • Quarterly Sales Dataset
  • Model Sales Share Dataset
  • Country-wise Share Dataset
  • apple_products.xlsx (450+ rows, 12+ features)
  • market penetration Dataset
  • Flipkart_Smartphones Datasets
